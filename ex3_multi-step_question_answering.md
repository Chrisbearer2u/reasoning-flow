1.  Select a multi-step question.

        "A sweater costs ₦25, and a pair of pants costs ₦15. If you pay with a ₦50 bill, how much change do you receive?"

2.  Create a prompt that asks the model to:

        # Restate the problem

            "A sweater is priced at ₦25 and a pair of pants costs ₦15. If you pay for both items using a ₦50 bill, how much change will you get back?"

        # Break it into smaller steps

            Here’s the problem broken down into smaller steps:

            A- Identify the cost of each item.

                    . Sweater = ₦25

                    . Pants = ₦15

            B- Find the total cost of both items.

                    . ₦25 + ₦15

            C- Add the amounts.

                    . ₦25 + ₦15 = ₦40

            D- Identify the amount paid.

                    . Paid = ₦50

            E- Subtract the total cost from the amount    paid to find the change.

                    . ₦50 − ₦40

            STEP 6- Calculate the change.

                    . ₦50 − ₦40 = ₦10

            So, the change received is ₦10.
